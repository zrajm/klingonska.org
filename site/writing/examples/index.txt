Some Pictures of {pIqaD}
========================
These are some pictures of the different styles of {pIqaD} that I have
encountered, mainly those used by [Nick Nicholas](#nicholas), [Roger
Cheesbro](#cheesbro) and [myself](#zrajm). It is my intention to write a more
comprehensive description of the Klingon writing system with detailed
information about different styles of longhand as used by klingonists and
Klingons around the galaxy. (Sooner or later.) The main research for this
article consists of these pictures, but as of now the pictures will have to
speak for themselves.

If you have anything that you wish to contribute, you are more than welcome to
contact [zrajm](mailto:zrajm@klingonska.org), and share what you've got. All
contributions will, of course, be fully credited.
