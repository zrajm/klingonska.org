{ghajwI'vaD qav'ap le' yIDIl.}
Pay the special rent to the owner.

{voHDajbo' qav'ap}
mortgage value

{voHDajbo' wellu' 200}
Mortgaged for 200.

{yervamvaD voHDajbo' wellu'chugh, 'echletHom Dopvam yIcha'}
Card must be turned this side up if property is mortgaged.

{HoSHal ghajchugh vay', mI' naghmey tIronmoH. wa'maHlogh boq'egh
mI' naghmey; chen qav'ap. ghajwI'vaD qav'ap yIDIl.}
If [energy source is] owned, throw dice and pay owner a total
ten times amount shown.

{Duj ghajchugh vay', cha'logh boq'egh qav'ap motlh; chen qav'ap le'.
ghajwI'vaD qav'ap le' yIDIl.}
If [ship is] owned, pay owner twice the rental to which they are
otherwise entitled.
